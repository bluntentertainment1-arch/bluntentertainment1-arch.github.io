Privacy Policy

Device Tuner Pro 26

Last updated: 28 December 2025

Device Tuner Pro 26 ("the App") provides system information, battery usage insights, photo management tools, and quick access to device settings. We value your privacy and are committed to transparency about how the App works and how data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

The App does not collect, store, or require personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location.

b) Device & Usage Information

The App may access system-provided information including:

  • Battery level and usage statistics
  • Running or background app information (as provided by the operating system)
  • Storage and media information related to photos selected by the user

This information is processed locally on the device and is not transmitted to our own servers.

2. How the App Works

Battery Monitoring

The App displays battery information provided by the operating system. It may offer tips and shortcuts to system settings. The App does not automatically modify system power behavior.

Game Mode

Game Mode provides a focused user experience by displaying usage information and offering quick access to relevant device settings. The App does not overclock hardware or modify system performance beyond user-initiated actions.

Photo Cleanup

Photo Cleanup allows users to view and manually select photos for deletion. All deletions require explicit user confirmation.

3. Permissions

The App requests permissions only when necessary:

  • Media / Storage Access – To display user-selected photos
  • Network Access – To support ads and basic app functionality
  • System Settings Access – To open system settings when requested

Permissions can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Device Tuner Pro 26 uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads.

AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data in order to provide, personalize, and measure advertising performance.

We do not have direct access to this advertising data. Data processing is governed by Google’s own privacy policies.

Learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. GDPR & UK GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, your personal data is processed in accordance with GDPR and UK GDPR requirements.

Advertising data for users in these regions is processed only after obtaining user consent. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time through the App’s consent or privacy settings, or via your device’s advertising preferences.

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data where applicable.

6. Data Sharing

The App does not sell or trade user data. Data may be processed by trusted third-party service providers such as Google AdMob solely for advertising and related services.

7. Data Security

The App operates primarily on-device. Any accessed information remains on the user’s device unless processed by third-party services as described above. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect data integrity.

8. Children’s Privacy

Device Tuner Pro 26 is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
