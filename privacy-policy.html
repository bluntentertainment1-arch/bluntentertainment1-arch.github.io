Privacy Policy

Device Tuner Pro 26

Last updated: 28 December 2025

Device Tuner Pro 26 ("the App") is designed to provide users with system information, battery usage insights, photo management tools, and quick access to device settings. We value your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding how the App works and how data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

Device Tuner Pro 26 does not collect, store, or share personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

b) Device & Usage Information

The App may access system-provided information including:

  • Battery level and usage statistics
  • Running or background app information (as provided by Android)
  • Storage and media information related to photos selected by the user

This information is processed locally on the device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. How the App Works

Battery Monitoring

The App displays battery information provided by the Android operating system. It may offer tips and shortcuts to system settings. The App does not automatically modify system power behavior.

Game Mode

Game Mode provides a focused user experience by displaying usage information and offering quick access to relevant device settings. The App does not overclock hardware or modify system performance beyond user-controlled actions.

Photo Cleanup

Photo Cleanup allows users to view and manually select photos for deletion. All file deletions require explicit user confirmation.

3. Permissions

The App requests permissions only when necessary:

  • Media / Storage Access – To display user-selected photos
  • Network Access – For basic app functionality
  • System Settings Access – To open Android system settings when requested

Permissions can be revoked at any time through device settings.

4. Data Sharing

Device Tuner Pro 26 does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. The App does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect personal data.

5. Data Security

The App operates primarily on-device. Any accessed information remains on the user’s device and is not uploaded to external servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

Device Tuner Pro 26 is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
